# Docketra Landing Page Positioning (May 2026)

## Positioning statement
Docketra is the **Company Brain and Work Execution OS for Indian professional firms**, starting with CS, CA, law, and compliance teams.

## What changed
The public landing page now leads with:
- Company Brain positioning for Indian professional firms.
- Work Execution OS framing grounded in current product behavior.
- Practical firm operations language (client memory + dockets + worklists + workbaskets + QC + reports + audit trails).

## Product reality guardrail
Docketra remains **Worklist-first operationally**:
- Worklist-first task execution
- Workbaskets and QC Workbaskets
- Dockets and docket lifecycle tracking
- Client and team management
- Work management and reports
- Client Fact Sheet / client memory direction
- Role-based access and audit trail foundations
- BYOS/storage transparency direction

## Messaging boundaries
- Do not position Docketra as a generic CRM/CMS/todo tool.
- Do not position Docketra as a Teams-like collaboration suite.
- Do not overclaim AI capabilities.
- Do not claim certifications (SOC2/ISO/DPDP) unless officially held and verified.

## AI language policy (public pages)
Use grounded phrasing such as:
- "Built for AI-assisted firm operations — without losing control"
- "Designed for"
- "Roadmap direction"

Do not claim fully live AI automation unless the capability is generally available and validated in product.

## CTA and route guardrails
Public CTAs must preserve existing auth/public entry points:
- Primary: `Create workspace` -> `/signup`
- Secondary: `Find workspace` -> `/find-workspace`

## Intended audience
- Practicing Company Secretary firms
- CA firms
- Law firms
- Compliance teams
- Small professional services firms in India
