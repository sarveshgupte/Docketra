# Docketra Landing Page Positioning (April 2026)

## Purpose
This document defines the current public landing-page messaging for Docketra so product, design, and engineering stay aligned on positioning and trust claims.

## Target audience
Docketra is currently positioned primarily for Indian professional-services and operations teams:
- CA firms
- CS firms
- Law firms
- Compliance and advisory teams
- Operations-heavy service businesses
- Multi-branch and multi-team firm operations

## Core value proposition
Docketra is a connected operating system for:
1. Getting clients
2. Managing clients
3. Executing work

In practical terms, Docketra combines three modules in one operating flow:
- **CMS** for demand capture and structured intake
- **CRM** for relationship and pipeline management
- **Tasks** for execution and delivery

## Core operating flow
`CMS -> CRM -> Tasks`

### CMS
- Landing pages
- Public forms
- Embedded forms on existing websites
- API intake for integrations
- Structured lead capture
- Intake queue

### CRM
- Lead pipeline
- List + pipeline view
- Owner assignment
- Follow-up tracking
- Notes/activity context
- Conversion tracking
- Client/account management orientation

### Tasks
- Dockets
- Internal tasks
- Workbaskets
- QC flow
- Firm-scoped routing
- Audit trail
- Operational queues

## Platform strengths to highlight
- Multi-tenant firm-aware model
- Role-based access
- Firm-scoped routing model
- Ongoing performance improvements
- Clear legal/compliance information pages (Terms, Privacy, Security, Acceptable Use)
- BYOS direction (Bring Your Own Storage)
- BYOAI direction (optional AI integrations without mandatory dependency)

## UX strengths to highlight
- Dashboard quick actions
- Better OTP/login experience
- Clearer forms and user feedback
- Realistic workflow visuals tied to product behavior

## Example workflows to use in copy
Use realistic examples relevant to Indian firms:
- GST filing
- ROC return
- Compliance review
- Client intake
- Internal approvals
- Operational follow-ups

## Messaging guardrails
- Keep language clear and non-jargony.
- Avoid fake enterprise, security, or legal claims.
- Do not imply billing/subscriptions are live.
- Tie each feature area to practical operational outcomes.
- Keep confidence high but avoid hype.

## Current pricing status (must remain accurate)
- Early access is currently free.
- Billing/subscriptions are not live yet.
- Copy should explicitly state testing phase and invite feedback.
